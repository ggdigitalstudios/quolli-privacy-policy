Privacy Policy for Quolli

Last updated: 13 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Quolli ("the App") handles information when you use the app.

Quolli is a party game app developed by GG Digital Studios.

1. Information We Collect

Quolli does not require account creation or user login.

The app may store limited information locally on your device in order to provide app features and save your preferences.

This may include:

  • language preferences
  • theme or visual settings
  • local game data
  • player names created during gameplay
  • locally stored gameplay-related counters or progress

2. How Information Is Used

The information stored by the app is used only to:

  • run the app correctly
  • save user preferences
  • improve the gameplay experience
  • keep locally created session data available on the device

3. In-App Purchases

Quolli may offer optional in-app purchases through Google Play in order to unlock premium game modes or features.

Purchases are processed by Google Play, not directly by Quolli. We do not store or process your payment card details.

4. Data Sharing

Quolli does not sell your personal data.

Quolli does not share personal information with third parties except where necessary for app distribution, purchase processing, legal compliance, or platform functionality provided by services such as Google Play.

5. Data Storage

Information used by Quolli is primarily stored locally on your device.

If you uninstall the app, locally stored data may be deleted from your device unless your device or platform keeps a backup.

6. Children's Privacy

Quolli is intended for adults and is not directed to children.

Some game modes may include content intended for mature audiences.

7. Your Choices

You can stop all collection of locally stored app data by uninstalling the app.

You may also reset app preferences or remove locally stored content by clearing the app data from your device settings, where available.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
